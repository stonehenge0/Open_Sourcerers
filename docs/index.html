About

This website can help you find the right charity to donate to! We have collected and tagged a database of 270 charities from a range of different charity evaluators. You can fill out the form and we will recommend you some charities based on your preferences. You can find the questionnaire here. The project was coded and designed by the people shown below. It is the final project for the Data Science module "Programming for Data Scientist" taught at the University of Göttingen. If you are interested in where our data comes from or seeing the source code, you can find answers on our help page
